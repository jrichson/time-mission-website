ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT

Time Mission Corp. is committed to making our website accessible to as many people as possible, regardless of ability or technology. We believe everyone should be able to step into the mission.

Our Standard

We target conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines cover a wide range of recommendations for making web content more accessible to people with visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive disabilities.

What We Do

Color & Contrast. We test foreground/background contrast ratios across the site and make adjustments where they fall below AA thresholds.

Keyboard Navigation. All interactive elements can be reached and operated using a keyboard alone.

Semantic HTML. We use proper headings, landmarks, and ARIA roles so assistive technology can interpret the page structure.

Alternative Text. We provide descriptive alt text for photos and decorative alt="" for purely visual elements.

Responsive Layout. Pages reflow at 320px width and scale to 200% without loss of content or function.

Known Limitations

We are continuing to improve. Current known issues include:

Hero Videos. Background videos are decorative, muted, and have poster frames, but do not include captions.

Motion. Some pages include scroll-triggered animations. These respect the prefers-reduced-motion setting on most browsers.

Assistance

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, or need information on this site in an alternative format, please contact us. We treat accessibility reports with priority and respond within three business days.

Email: accessibility@timemission.com

Formal Complaints

We aim to resolve accessibility issues directly and quickly. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may file a complaint with the appropriate regulatory authority for your jurisdiction (e.g., the U.S. Department of Justice for ADA-related concerns, or national enforcement bodies for EN 301 549 in the EU).

Updates to This Statement

We will revise this statement as our site evolves and as we resolve known issues.
