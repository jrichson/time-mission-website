Where Competition Becomes Adventure

Time Mission transforms ordinary entertainment into extraordinary experiences. We create immersive gaming arenas where teams of 2-5 players tackle 25+ themed challenges, racing against time and each other across 7 global locations.

Our brand captures the electricity of competition, the magic of themed immersion, and the joy of shared victory. Every touchpoint—from digital to physical—should feel like stepping through a portal into adventure.

01 — Logo System

THE MARK

Our wordmark represents the fusion of time-pressure and mission-driven adventure. The bold, geometric letterforms convey strength and precision while remaining approachable and modern.

Mark — Social & Favicons

✓ Do

  • Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "T"
  • Use on high-contrast backgrounds for maximum legibility
  • Scale proportionally without distortion
  • Use the gradient mark for digital-first applications
  • Minimum size: 120px width for digital, 30mm for print

✗ Don't

  • Stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
  • Change the letterforms or spacing
  • Place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
  • Add effects like drop shadows or outlines
  • Use colors outside the approved palette

04 — Voice & Tone

HOW WE SPEAK

Our voice is confident and energizing, speaking to teams ready for adventure. We're the guide who pumps you up before the challenge begins.

⚡

ENERGETIC

We pulse with excitement. Our words should make hearts race and adrenaline surge. Short, punchy sentences. Active voice. Urgency without anxiety.

🎯

DIRECT

No fluff, no filler. Every word earns its place. We respect our audience's time and intelligence. Clear instructions, bold claims, confident statements.

🌟

INVITING

We welcome everyone to the challenge. Inclusive language, no gatekeeping. First-timers and veterans alike feel the call to adventure.

🏆

TRIUMPHANT

We celebrate victory and growth. Players leave feeling accomplished. Our language builds anticipation and delivers satisfaction.

Writing Examples

Don't
"Welcome to our entertainment facility where you and your group can participate in various challenge-based activities designed to test different skill sets."
Do
"Your team. 25 challenges. 90 minutes. Think fast. Move faster. Conquer together."
Don't
"Please proceed to select your preferred time slot and number of participants for your reservation request."
Do
"Ready to play? Pick your time, grab your team, and let's go."
Don't
"The Glitch Matrix experience offers a digitally-themed environment with technology-focused puzzles."
Do
"Glitch Matrix: Hack the simulation. Bend the code. Escape before reality crashes."
05 — Visual Elements

VISUAL LANGUAGE

Beyond colors and type, these elements create the immersive atmosphere that defines the Time Mission experience.

Photography Style

Dynamic action shots with dramatic lighting. High contrast, saturated colors. Motion blur acceptable for energy. Teams in action, not posed. Authentic emotion and competition.

Motion Principles

Smooth easing with energetic timing. Staggered reveals create rhythm. Hover states respond instantly. Scroll animations feel natural. Never distracting, always purposeful.

Iconography

Geometric, bold line icons with 2px stroke. Rounded corners for approachability. Consistent optical sizing. Color icons for category differentiation only.

Patterns & Textures

Subtle noise overlay (2-3% opacity) for depth. Gradient glows for emphasis. Geometric grids for backgrounds. Angular lines suggest speed and direction.
