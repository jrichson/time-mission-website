## Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPG for photos, SVG for logos, MP4 for video
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1200px wide for photos, 1920x1080 for hero
- **Aspect ratios**:
  - Hero: 16:9
  - Experience cards: 3:2 or 4:3
  - Team photos: 16:9 or 3:2
- **File size**: Keep images under 500KB each (I can compress if needed)
