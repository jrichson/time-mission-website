Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Do gift cards expire?

No! Time Mission gift cards never expire. The recipient can use them whenever they're ready for their adventure.

Can gift cards be used at any location?

Yes! Gift cards are valid at all Time Mission locations in the US and internationally.

What can gift cards be used for?

Gift cards can be used for general admission tickets, birthday party packages, corporate events, and any merchandise at our locations.

Can I check my gift card balance?

Yes! You can check your balance at any location or by contacting our customer service team at info@timemission.com.

Can I get a refund on a gift card?

Gift cards are non-refundable but they never expire, so the recipient can use them whenever they're ready!
