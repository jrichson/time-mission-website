COOKIE POLICY

This Cookie Policy explains how Time Mission Corp. ("Time Mission," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit timemission.com. It works alongside our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They let the site remember your preferences, keep you signed in, measure traffic, and support marketing.

Categories We Use

Strictly Necessary. Required for the site to function — page navigation, security, and remembering your selected location. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics & Performance. Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. Examples: Google Analytics 4, Microsoft Clarity.

Advertising & Marketing. Used to measure the effectiveness of our ads and show you relevant content on other platforms. Examples: Meta (Facebook) Pixel, Google Ads conversion tag, TikTok Pixel.

Functional. Remember choices you make (like your preferred location) to personalize your experience.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by services we use — such as Google, Meta, and TikTok. We do not control these cookies directly. Review each provider's policy for details on what they collect.

Your Choices

Cookie Banner. When you first visit, you can accept or reject non-essential cookie categories.

Browser Controls. Every major browser lets you block, delete, or be prompted about cookies. Disabling some may affect site functionality.

Opt Out of Advertising. Visit aboutads.info or youronlinechoices.eu (EU) to opt out of interest-based advertising.

Do Not Track

Our site does not currently respond to browser "Do Not Track" signals, because no uniform standard exists. We instead rely on the cookie banner and your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our practices or the tools we use change. The "Last Updated" date below reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about our cookie practices? Email info@timemission.com.
