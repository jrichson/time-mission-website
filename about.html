THE EXPERIENCE

WHAT IS TIME MISSION?

Time Mission is an immersive social gaming experience where teams of 2-5 compete through 25+ interactive challenge rooms. Every room is a different game — from brain-bending puzzles to full-body physical challenges. There are no screens, no headsets, and no sitting around. You are the game.

Choose your session length, pick your missions, and play as many rooms as you can. Each challenge lasts just a few minutes, so you can keep moving, keep competing, and keep the energy high.

Whether it's a birthday, a date night, a team outing, or just a crew that wants to see who's really the best — Time Mission is where you come to play.

PLAN YOUR VISIT

BEFORE YOU ARRIVE

BOOK IN ADVANCE

We recommend booking ahead to guarantee your spot. Walk-ins are welcome but subject to availability.

DRESS TO PLAY

Wear comfortable clothes you can run, jump, and crawl in. Closed-toe shoes with flat soles required.

ARRIVE EARLY

Show up 10-15 minutes before your session to check in and get briefed. Don't miss a single second.

BRING YOUR CREW

Teams of 2-5 players per challenge. The more people, the more fun — and the more rooms you can conquer.
