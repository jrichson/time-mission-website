Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

What's the minimum headcount for a buyout?

Varies by location, but most buyouts start around 40–60 guests. Send us your size and we'll confirm what works at your chosen venue.

How is buyout pricing structured?

Custom pricing based on venue, date, duration, and headcount. Request a quote and we'll send a detailed proposal.

Can we add custom branding or signage?

Yes, most buyouts include options for branded signage, custom welcome experiences, and sometimes mission personalization. Let us know what you have in mind.

Is catering available?

Catering and bar options vary by location. Some venues have full in-house packages, others work with outside caterers. We'll walk you through what's available when you book.

What hours are buyouts available?

Most locations can accommodate buyouts during standard operating hours plus extended or after-hours slots. Extended hours may carry additional cost.

Do you handle AV, entertainment, or activations?

We can coordinate AV basics in-house and work with outside vendors for larger activations, entertainment, and custom experiences. Let's talk through what the event needs.

Ready to take over the venue?

Tell us your headcount, date, and event type, we'll build a buyout plan around it.
