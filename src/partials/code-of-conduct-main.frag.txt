        <h2>Smoking Policy</h2>
        <p>Smoking is strictly prohibited at all Time Mission locations. This includes the use of vaporizers and electronic cigarettes.</p>

        <h2>Language and Behavior</h2>
        <p>Foul language, inappropriate gestures, and disrespectful behavior are prohibited on the premises. Management reserves the right to ask guests to leave without refund for inappropriate behavior.</p>

        <h2>Outside Food and Beverage</h2>
        <p>Guests are not permitted to bring outside food or beverages into Time Mission locations unless previously authorized by management.</p>

        <h2>Dress Code</h2>
        <p>We recommend wearing comfortable athletic wear and sneakers, as Time Mission challenges involve running, jumping, crawling, and climbing. Please avoid wearing baggy or bulky clothing, as it may restrict movement, and high heels are not permitted as they can damage our game equipment.</p>
        <p><strong>Proper Attire:</strong> Guests wearing torn or visibly soiled clothing may not be admitted at the discretion of management.</p>
        <p><strong>Prohibited Attire:</strong> Clothing, jewelry, or visible tattoos with offensive language, symbols, or evidence of gang affiliation are not permitted.</p>
        <p><strong>Exposure:</strong> Clothing that exposes underwear or excessive portions of skin may be considered inappropriate for a family-friendly environment.</p>

        <h2>Weapons Policy</h2>
        <p>There is no "open carry" of firearms permitted at Time Mission locations, except by sworn law enforcement personnel.</p>

        <h2>Filming and Photography Policy</h2>
        <p>We encourage guests to take photos and videos of their Time Mission experience! Feel free to tag us on social media (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook: @TimeMissionHQ).</p>
        <p><strong>Personal Use:</strong> Guests are welcome to take photos or videos for personal use.</p>
        <p><strong>Commercial Use:</strong> Permission is required for any filming or photography intended for commercial use or journalism.</p>
        <p><strong>Consent:</strong> Filming or photography that captures other guests or staff members requires their express permission.</p>
        <p><strong>Management Rights:</strong> Management reserves the right to revoke permission for filming or photography at any time for any reason.</p>
